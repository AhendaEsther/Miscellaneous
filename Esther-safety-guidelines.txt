Importance of fire training and Safeguarding policy.
There is no bullying allowed on campus and every student should adhere to this rule.
Incase of a fire emergency put on the fire alarm to alert others and move to a safe space
Always gather at the fire assembly and wait for further instructions from your mentors.
Incase of sexual harassment,a student should report immediately because thats a violation of their rights.
Always be mindful of electronic devices and remember to turn them off when not in use to avoid incidences of fire.
